ATM app design

The design of this app design was done as a group work of four members. ATM is an Italian main transportation source that allows customers to purchase their tickets though ATM points and also app.

Our goal was to develop an easy, fast, convenient, and user friendly app that will solve problems as quick and as ef ciently as possible. To do that we concentrated on simplicity, avaliability and color codes, along with typography and easy language to guide our customers though our design and help them move with their day with comfort and a smile on their faces.
