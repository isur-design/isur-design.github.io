Hyphen Press Catalogue and Almanack

Hyphen Press buplishes books and catalogues on design topics. The task was to redesign one of their catalogues using only fonts and typography. As the catalogue combined important names of people, information, and documents from earlier times till now the concept of the project was time.

The main inspiration of this design is typewriter. Using mono spaced font gave it even more look of being similar to typewritten documents saved from a long time. Bold, light, and regular weight of fonts created a good combination adding more to the design.
