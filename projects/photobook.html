Eternal Beauty Photobook

Francesca Woodman was an amazing American photographer who unfortunately got world famous and understood only after her suicide. Her images contained of nudity, many movements which gave less clarity to images, mirrors, and beauty.

The design concept of this photobook was based on combination of Francesca Woodman’s quotes, and Brother’s Grimm story book “Snow White”. The Eternal Beauty is following the deaths and ressuractions of Snow White, and the fortunes of Mirror. Two stories of different kind were combined with the design elements of mirror effects and transparency, along with text located inside the pages with the help of french binding to deliver create a mysterious and fascinating stories of Francesca Woodman’s photography.
