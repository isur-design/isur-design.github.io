Rete design localizer

Rete is an app helping new and professional designers connect with master craftsman and historian. In this app they can see their work, comment, contact, connect and collaborate with them. Rete is a networking app with huge potential for the communication and interior design.

All actors on a design project can use the platform to work collaboratively and to interact with professionals and experts in the field of Italian Interior Design in real time. Interior design students and professionals can learn about and source materials for projects requiring Italian design expertise or Italian made materials.
