Aldea ecovillage branding

Aldea book is dedicated to the problems of South America’s waters. The idea was to create a leasure and animal protection eco-village. Aldea meaning “village” from Spanish translation, was our main concept. However, after a long research we realized the lack of eco-villages associated with waters. Therefore, believing that this idea can be interesting as a concept and color pallates, we designed an identity that can deliver the true colors of oceans.

Group project/Role: Idea, development, icon design, mockups
